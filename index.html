About Me

Hello! I'm a 23-year-old student currently pursuing a Bachelors of Integrated Studies undergraduate degree, and I'm in my final year of college. I grew up in Michigan with time split between Traverse City and Milford, I have always been passionate about technology and its vast possibilities.

Presently, I have the privilege to work at the Rochester Hills Public Library as an IT specialist. This role has sharpened my technical skills and has solidified my career goal: becoming a full stack developer. I'm driven by the desire to create seamless and intuitive digital experiences for users, and I'm always eager to learn and grow in the realm of web development.
